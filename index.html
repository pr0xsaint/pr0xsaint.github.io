Seasonal Cycles

We will not launch until the server is 85% ready. Once we reach that milestone, Season 0 begins.

Every Season lasts **3 months**. Afterward, your character migrates to the "Standard" league to play forever, while a fresh Season begins. Every new Season brings a balance patch dependent on the previous meta.

The Gimmick

Every season has a unique twist. For Season 1, our goal is to break your attachment to pixels.

You will lose items. We want you to stop worshipping your gear and start using it. You will get used to the loss, you will adapt, and eventually, you will love the thrill. And if not... oh well.
